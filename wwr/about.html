At Whitewater Rafting Co., our mission is to deliver thrilling, safe, and unforgettable adventures on the rapids. We believe in the power of nature to inspire and challenge, and we're here to guide you every splash of the way!

History

Founded in 1985 by a group of river enthusiasts, Whitewater Rafting Co. started with just one raft and a dream. Today, we're the leading provider of adrenaline-pumping experiences across the Rocky Mountains, with over 50,000 satisfied adventurers.
