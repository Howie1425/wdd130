Choose Your Adventure

Our Rafting Trips

We offer a variety of thrilling whitewater experiences suitable for all ages and skill levels. Explore nature, build teamwork, or just chase the rush — there’s a trip for everyone.

Family Float Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families with children, this gentle ride provides fun with calm waters and beautiful river scenery.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Class I–II Rapids
  • Guided tour + life jackets + helmets

Half-Day Whitewater

A great balance of adventure and relaxation. Paddle through exciting rapids while enjoying quick breaks and scenic stops.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Class III–IV Rapids
  • Guide, gear, snack stop included

Full-Day Extreme Challenge

Designed for thrill-seekers, this intense full-day experience tackles the wildest rapids. Physical fitness required!

  • Duration: 7 hours
  • Class IV–V Rapids
  • All safety gear, lunch, and shuttle included

What’s Included

Every trip includes professional guides and top-quality equipment for your safety and comfort:

  • ✔ Life Jackets (PFDs)
  • ✔ Helmets
  • ✔ Paddle & Raft
  • ✔ Dry Bags (for full-day trips)
  • ✔ Wetsuits (when necessary)
